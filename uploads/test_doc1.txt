The capital of France is Paris. The city is known for its beautiful architecture and the Eiffel Tower.
The population of Paris is approximately 2.2 million people in the city proper.

The largest planet in our solar system is Jupiter. It has a mass of 1.9 x 10^27 kilograms and is composed mainly of hydrogen and helium.
Jupiter has 79 known moons, with the four largest being Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
